## Upload and Visualize

Bring your emotes and badges to life! With Badge Tester for Twitch, you can effortlessly
upload your custom emotes and images, and instantly see how they would look in Twitch chat. Preview, fine-tune, and perfect your creations with ease.

## Perfect for Artists and Streamers

Artists and streamers, rejoice! Badge Tester for Twitch lets you showcase your
artistic brilliance and experiment with captivating designs, all without the need to upload anything to Twitch.

## Privacy First

Your creations are for your eyes only. Badge Tester for Twitch prioritizes your privacy and keeps all images on your device.

We take the security of your data seriously. Badge Tester for Twitch does not store or process any user data outside of your device. Your emotes, images, and testing configurations are confined to the App on your device.
