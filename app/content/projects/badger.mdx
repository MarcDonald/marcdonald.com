## The Boring Stuff

### Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how Badger ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, maintains, and protects information obtained from users of the Badger mobile application ("App").
By using Badger, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

#### Information Collection and Usage:

Badger is designed with privacy in mind. No data is ever transmitted from your device to our servers or any external entities. The App operates solely on your local device, and all image uploads and emote testing occur within the confines of your device.

#### In-App Purchases and Payments:

Badger uses Apple's In-App Purchase system for certain features or services within the App. Apple's In-App Purchase system handles the transaction and billing details securely. We do not have access to or store any payment information. For information regarding the collection and processing of payment data, please refer to Apple's privacy policy.

#### Permissions:

Badger does not request, access, or store any personal information, device information, or user data. The App only requires the necessary permissions to function within the local environment of your device.

#### Image Uploads:

When you use Badger to upload images for emote testing, these images remain on your device and are not transmitted to any external servers or third-party services.

#### Data Security:

We take the security of your data seriously. Badger does not store or process any user data outside of your device. Your emotes, images, and testing configurations are confined to the App on your device.

#### Changes to this Privacy Policy:

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users will be notified of any changes through a prominent notice within the App. We encourage users to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or Badger, please contact us at
